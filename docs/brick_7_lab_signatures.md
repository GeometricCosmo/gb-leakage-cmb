## Part A: The Core Laboratory Mechanism

If the radion-EM coupling 
\[
\mathcal{L}_{\rm int} = -\frac{\lambda}{M_5^{3/2}} r(x) \, (\partial_\mu \phi)^2
\]
is physical, then **sharp electromagnetic field gradients** on the brane can induce small, transient radion fluctuations.

In the laboratory this could manifest as:
- Local modification of effective gravitational strength
- Anomalous forces or momentum transfer
- Minute changes in inertia or weight

---

## Part B: Most Promising Signature — Asymmetric Capacitors in Vacuum

**Setup**:
- High-voltage asymmetric capacitor (Biefeld-Brown type geometry)
- Operated in high vacuum (< 10⁻⁵ Torr)
- Sharp voltage pulses or high-frequency AC

**Predicted Effect** (highly tentative):
- Small anomalous thrust or weight change correlated with voltage gradient sharpness
- Force scale estimate: 10⁻⁶ to 10⁻³ N (depending on λ coupling strength and voltage)
- Effect should disappear in symmetric geometries or when radion excitation is suppressed

**Why this geometry?**
Asymmetric capacitors naturally create strong, localized ∇(E²) terms → strong source for the radion via the coupling.

## Part C: Other Possible Signatures

1. **Precision Force Measurements**
   - Ultra-sensitive torsion balances or interferometers near high-voltage pulsed systems
   - Look for transient gravitational anomalies correlated with EM activity

2. **Capacitance / Dielectric Anomalies**
   - Frequency-dependent deviations in measured capacitance under high gradient conditions

3. **Refractive Index Fluctuations**
   - Laser interferometry detecting tiny, transient changes in local spacetime metric

4. **Resonance Effects**
   - If the radion has a natural mass/frequency, certain EM frequencies might produce enhanced effects

## Part D: Falsification Criteria

The model would be seriously challenged if:
- Careful, replicated experiments with asymmetric capacitors in vacuum show **null results** down to 10⁻⁷ N sensitivity
- No correlation is found between force anomalies and sharpness of EM gradients
- Effects claimed in literature are shown to be purely ionic wind, electrostatic, or systematic
